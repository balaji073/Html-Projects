A crypto airdrop is a marketing strategy where a cryptocurrency project or company sends a free or low-cost token or coin to multiple digital wallets. The goal is to increase awareness of the cryptocurrency, encourage its adoption, and gain new followers. Airdrops are often associated with the launch of a new cryptocurrency or DeFi protocol. They can also be used to reward early participants or existing customers.

Here are some things to know about crypto airdrops:

How they work

  • Companies may collect wallet addresses or email addresses from interested parties.
  • They may also use a snapshot to identify who meets certain criteria.
  • For example, a snapshot could identify all addresses that held at least 1,000 tokens as of a specific time.
